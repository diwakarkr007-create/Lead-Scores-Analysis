## X-Education-Lead-Scores-Analysis

# OVERVIEW
This repository contains the analysis and predictive modeling for X Education, an online education company that sells courses to industry professionals. The primary goal of this project is to build a lead scoring model to identify high-potential leads and improve the company's overall sales conversion rate.
#Problem Statement
X Education attracts a large number of professionals to its website daily through various marketing channels, including search engines, partner websites, social media, and past referrals. Visitors who browse courses, watch videos, and fill out forms with their contact information are classified as leads.
Once acquired, the sales team attempts to convert these leads into paying customers through calls and emails. However, the current process is highly inefficient. The typical lead-to-sale conversion rate is only around 30%. This means if the company acquires 100 leads, only about 30 are successfully converted, leading to wasted time and effort on leads that will never buy.

# Project Objective
To make the sales process more efficient, X Education wants to identify the most promising leads—referred to as 'Hot Leads'.
By successfully building a model to identify this specific set of high-probability leads, the company can:
Increase the Conversion Rate: Ensure the sales team prioritizes individuals with genuine interest.
Optimize Resource Allocation: Allow the sales team to focus on communicating with potential buyers rather than making cold calls to the entire lead pool.
